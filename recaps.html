This page contains recaps from all of our past sessions. More recent sessions are closer to the top.

13 February, 2026 - Ses. 26

  • The party explored the city graveyard and learned of some old catacombs.
  • Queen Dahlia Nyxlanyss requested an audience with the party which the party accepted.
  • During the meeting, Dahlia asked the party to join her for dinner, which they accepted.
  • The party went to the Silver Hammer and met Mintho. There, they upgraded 4 weapons to +2 weapons:
    • Drangea: rapier, light crossbow
    • Sebastian: quarterstaff
    • Lyrix: longbow
  • The party got an unofficial quest to get an ancient vampire's remains from the catacombs to upgrade their blood pendants.
  • The party got two potions of healing from the potion shop (Lyrix threatened the shop owner multiple times).
  • Hark studied the Forbidden Tome and learned a new ability: Quick Cast.
  • Sebastian made a salad for the dinner, and Drangea made a desert.
  • The party's weapons, armor, and foci were removed and they entered the dinner (Queen Dahlia was wearing the "Mantle of Nyxlanyss," actually Nyxeram).
  • During the dinner, Lyrix repeatedly offended the queen and was carried out.
  • Queen Dahlia discussed her efforts toward the city (including relocating homeless people and looking into affordable healthcare).
  • After the dinner, she left a ruby behind for the party; Hark took 25gp and Sebastian spread out the rest in silver throughout the Slums.
  • Before bed, Drangea performed a rite for "Aster's" sword, and Hark met with secret followers of Oghma asking him to obtain the Song of Fading Light.
  • Sebastian learned that the rebellion in his hometown was going poorly due to rampant spreading of disease.
  • The next day, the party obtained their enhanced weapons and entered the Nyxlanyss Catacombs.
  • Nyxeram taunted them for a few minutes before setting warriors and wizards afflicted with Dark Miasma onto them.
  • During the fight, Hark was polymorphed into a Giant Ape and destroyed the enemies with ease.

30 January, 2026 - Ses. 25

  • The party rested for the night after the battle with Nyxeram.
  • In the morning, the party found that the construct that controlled the elevator had been destroyed, meaning the elevator couldn't be used normally.
  • After some examining and experimenting, the characters managed to rig the elevator to go up and down via a pulley system.
  • After 2 hours and 12 minutes of lowering, the party reached the bottom of the chasm; they found that their darkvision wasn't working and had to rely on the light cantrip to see.
  • After hearing something clicking in the dark for a few minutes, they were attacked by darkened giant spiders and a shadow mangler; the characters ultimately prevailed.
  • Continuing on, they found the cave leaking some red liquid; they were careful to avoid touching it when necessary.
  • The party ran into Nyxeram who mentioned the red liquid to be water with iron in it.
  • Nyxeram also told the party a bit about Nyxlanyss and the Adventurers Culpability Act.
  • The party reached the city and were stopped by some guards; a failed attempt to smuggle in a Darkblood fruit resulted in Hark being arrested for the night.
  • After doing some exploring, the party rested for the night; Lyrix got super drunk and ended up in jail with Hark.
  • Hark and Lyrix were bailed out for 300 gp, and the party continued exploring Nyxlanyss.
  • Lyrix and Sebastian went to the slums and attempted to spread some cheer, with relatively low success.
  • Hark and Drangea went to the residential district and spoke with some of the people there, learning that they were a little bit racist and believed that Queen Dahlia Nyxlanyss would turn things around for the city.
  • The party met up at the graveyard where the session ended.
