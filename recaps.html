12 June, 2026 - Ses. 40

28 Leafdusk

  • Annabel continued training with her whip, quickly gaining mastery over it.
  • Ned and Drangea arrived with the party; after some discussion, they concluded he was real.
  • Ned mentioned having some soldiers ready to hold off the city's wardens as soon as the party is ready to take on Queen Dahlia.
  • Drangea discussed her research into Dark Miasma, mentioning her knowledge of how to taint a pool of water.
  • The party briefly visited the Temple of the Archons where Annabel got the druidic focus of Joanna the Wise from Cernunnos.
  • They attempted to form Dark Miasma in the Catacombs, but Osiris (the Archon of death) warned them against doing so, so they went to a small pool near their encampment.
  • After discussing some dark memories, the pool was filled with Dark Miasma; Drangea entered first but was told to grab her allies.
  • After 100 minutes of wandering, she made her way out and brought the rest of the party in.
  • After accepting an appointment at the monastery/hospital, the party waited for several minutes, occasionally seeing some familiar faces before getting called in.
  • After some discussion with the High Priestess Liliana, Drangea led the party to the shrine of Eilistraee; she received a response in a language she did not undersatand before leaving Aster's sword behind.
  • After more exploring, the party found a basement; within, a statue of Eilistraee dripped with blood; the blood came to life before attacking the party.
  • The necrichor used its blood puppeteering to force Annabel to attack the party while corrosive miasma attacked and corroded weapons and armor alike.
  • The party took a short rest after the battle, reparing their gear in the process.
  • Along their path, they ran into Ned who accompanied them; going down some stairs, Aster beckoned them forward.
  • At the bottom of the stairs, a large ooze made of melting faces attacked, controlling the fake Ned and Aster.
  • During the fight, it continued extruding humanoid forms of the party and people they knew, using them to attack and drain memories.
  • After being destroyed, the party found a swirling vortex of Dark Miasma with images of their dark memories within waiting to be stabilized.
  • Annabel was able to partially stabilize it by apologizing for leaving behind her kingdom for Sebastian.
  • Drangea was able to stabilize it further by apologizing for abandoning Aster to the monastery for her personal benefits.
  • The session ended with Hark trying to decide what he should do.
