Short Recaps

This page contains shortened recap from all of our past sessions. More recent sessions are closer to the top.

Detailed Recaps

10 April, 2026 - Ses. 33

Annabel investigated the city while the party rested. Ned tasked our heroes with blackmailing John Guinnessberg, an advisor to the Queen. After some personal business, the party rested for the night, waking up to the Amethyst Rain. While Hark and Drangea searched the catacombs, Annabel made a couple deals with Queen Dahlia in disguise of her brother. Hark got a job disguised as a warden after beating up a recruiter and was assigned to the prison. Annabel and Drangea explored the spire, learning of the prison structure. They found and blackmailed Guinnessberg and went searching for the Queen's personal library. She angered a warden and took the elevator down to delay being brought before the Queen. Drangea broke into the library, triggering alarm bells, and after some attempts to escape with Hark's help, she jumped out the window. The Queen's wizard, Melchor, captured Drangea and interrogated her, compromising the hideout's location. Hark and Annabel went to the catacombs to hide and rest.

3 April, 2026 - Ses. 32

Hark and Sebastian went to the Farming District to find its song fragment. Drangea woke up and stealthily found the party, and they found the fragment. They took a brief stop at the temple, and arrived at the blacksmith. Lyrix was guided there as well. They found the final fragment in the First Guildhouse, and made their way back to the hideout. Along the way, Nyxeram attacked and kidnapped Sebastian. Annabel was guided by Cernunnos to Nyxlanyss where she met the party and together, they started planning their next steps.

27 March, 2026 - Ses. 31

Our heroes discovered that the corpses in the tavern ruins had been reanimated, and so the party put them down, burning the tavern afterwards. The party found Sebastian's doppelganger in the Slums, where Drangea shot both and dispatched the doppelganger. Drangea was taken in for questioning before being released. The party discussed leaving the city behind after Nyxeram offered to save his home kingdom. Hark and Lyrix found more of the Song of Fading Light. Lyrix took an elixir of darkblood and while she and Sebastian cause chaos in the streets, Hark and Drangea broke into the Queen's bedroom and stole another song fragment. They were hidden by an ally named Ned who mentioned that Sebastian's sending stone was probably tampered with by Nyxeram. They rested for the night there.

20 March, 2026 - Ses. 30

While eating breakfast, dark monsters attacked our heroes, killing many innocent people in the inn. Sebastian and Lyrix went to the temple and got drunk at a tavern while Hark and Drangea snuck into the Spire and obtained a book on sightings of Nyxeram. The party was summoned to Queen Dahlia's chambers where they discussed their unauthorized quest for Mintho and she created a new law forbidding unauthorized quest. She discussed Sebastian's doppelganger before sending them off. Hark used the Forbidden Tome and Talisman to gain new power while Lyrix resisted Nyxeram's temptations of Darkblood fruit.

6 March, 2026 - Ses. 29

Hark and Lyrix found a black pouch with an evil talisman inside within the Forbidden Crypt. They couldn't reseal the crypt, so they took the talisman with them. They fought a darkened flameskull during which Drangea returned. While exploring, they learned more about the Talisman of Ultimate Evil. After fighting more undead, they arrived at the heart of the catacombs. Nyxeram revived the stolen body of Aster with Dark Miasma, and after a difficult fight, they put him to rest for good. Upon obtaining the vampiric remains, they brought it to Mintho who upgraded their pendants and rested for the night.

27 February, 2026 - Ses. 28

On the previous day, Sebastian and Lyrix were attending personal business. The party met up to search for clues about the doppelganger. They uncovered some information about the doppelganger's actions in the farms. They returned to the catacombs where they fought a darkened version of Vassamos the cambion. Nyxeram offered to send Sebastian back home to help with his rebellion but was refused. After fighting some more monsters, they faced a banshee for the key to the sealed chamber. They then opened the door to the Forbidden Crypt.

20 February, 2026 - Ses. 27

Hark and Drangea continued into the catacombs, facing off more undead. They found a locked and sealed door guarding a great evil. Hark got some plate armor which he got fitted by Mintho. They investigated rumors of someone who sounded a lot like Sebastian causing mischief, and they then returned to the catacombs. There, they fought more undead and met the spirit of the First Queen of Nyxlanyss in a platinum coffin who chastized them for hiding a body in and throwing up in her tomb. After cleaning up, they found the heart of the catacombs where Nyxeram kicked them out due to them being unready to enter. The party took a long rest.

13 February, 2026 - Ses. 26

Our heroes met Queen Dahlia who invited them to dinner. After upgrading some weapons to +2 weapons, they got an unofficial quest from Mintho the blacksmith to upgrade their blood pendants. Hark studied the Forbidden Tome, gaining new powers. At the dinner, Lyrix was carried out after offending the queen. Dahlia discussed her efforts toward the city and the characters performed various tasks before going to bed. They pickd up their weapons and entered the Nyxlanyss Catacombs where Nyxram taunted them for a few minutes. They fought some dark warriors and wizards, during which Hark was polymorphed into a Giant Ape.

30 January, 2026 - Ses. 25

Our heroes rested after the battle with Nyxeram. After some experimenting, they rigged the elevator to go up and down via pulley. After a couple of hours of descending, they arrived at the bottom, finding their darkvision not working. They heard something following them fought a shadow mangler and giant spiders before continuing on. Nyxeram spoke with them again, discussing Nyxlanyss and the Adventurers Culpability Act. Upon reaching the city, Hark was arrested for attempting to smuggle in a Darkblood fruit. While the party rested for the night, Lyrix got super drunk and ended up in jail with Hark. They were bailed out the next day, and they learned about the people of Nyxlanyss.

23 January, 2026 - Ses. 24

The party rested in the grove and returned to the Silverlight Sanctuary. They found a fellow traveler dead with a mysterious note on her body. Lyrix was forcefully cured of her infection, and the party headed North towards the elevator down. They fought some darkened monsters and arrived at the elevator. There, they saw a shadowy figure named Nyxeram who poked at the characters' insecurities. After failing to turn back the party, they fought each other (the party leveled up just before). Drangea woke up in time for battle, and after a difficult fight, the party prevailed with Nyxeram giving Hark the Forbidden Tome and fled down the chasm.

Level 8 Attained.

16 January, 2026 - Ses. 23

Our heroes arrived in a graveyard and rested. Aster was revived as a revenant of Eilistraee, upon which they set out. Lyrix consumed Darkblood fruits and fought some monsters influenced by them. She became fully infected after consuming their juices. Upon arriving at the Grove, they fought Drangea who created mirror images to help fight. After Drangea was beaten down, the heart was sacrificed and Drangea purified. Upon Drangea's revival, Aster returned to the afterlife forevermore.

9 January, 2026 - Ses. 22

Aster got lost after arriving at the Sanctuary. There, our heroes bought some supplies. They spoke with Priestess Valanthe who told them that the Rotlord's Heart was the key to saving Drangea from the Dark Miasma. After resting for the night, they traversed the mists to reach the rotting village. They fought a fully corrupted Michael the Rotlord, defeating him after Aster arrived mid-fight. They used holy water to purify the heart. The party learned that an ancient tome was stolen, although she didn't know who the culprit was. They then set out for the grove where Drangea resided. Tenebris attacked them along the way, and Aster, unable to escape, sacrificed himself to save the party.

12 December, 2025 - Ses. 21

Our heroes investigated the dark pool further, discovering it could create visions from memories. Sebastian and Lyrix's memories combined to create a forested city, eventually escaping. Aster spoke with an entity formed from his memories and the lake. They then narrowly escaped from Tenebris, meeting a living flame along the way and resting just outside the East entrance of the border caves. Exploring further, they found a crystal hut, a waterfall clearing, a dark demonic wolf, a bog corpse, and another dark fountain. They arrived at the mists surrounding the Silverlight Sanctuary which Aster was able to navigate, gaining access to the small village.

5 December, 2025 - Ses. 20

Our heroes looted Agatha's hut, gaining a mace of disruption and a wand of magic missiles crafted from her finger. The party rested in her hut and found a map of the Fumewoods. They traveled to a hidden passage in the North where they fought some bandits. While fighting, Tenebris, the Ancient Darkrender attacked and the party fled. In the caverns, they met John who gave each charcter a "self-improvement quest." Exploring further, they beat a gameshow host's game, gaining some pocket glitter. Our heroes then found a pool of dark water which allowed them to speak to Drangea, learning her location of the Giant Glittergrove.

14 November, 2025 - Ses. 19

A coin tossed into the birdbath took on a humanoid form to attack our heroes, being defeated after a battle. The party then made their way to Agatha's hut where they had tea before attacking her. She disappeared to her secret lair while leaving demons to distract our heroes. Hark regained his lost health as well as some Abyssal documents. They found the secret passage in her cauldron and descended down, facing her and her demon warriors. Hark dealt the killing blow, finishing her off for good.

7 November, 2025 - Ses. 18

Agatha showed up to offer Drangea's return for a price, leaving after being rejected. Drangea's brother, Aster, arrived to help with finding her. While exploring, the party passed by a fire cult and fought some stone cursed, resulting in Aster being petrified. The party rested while he recovered and spoke with Agatha by a clear pool where they rested for the night. They found a bird bath filled with black liquid with a connection to Drangea.

10 October, 2025 - Ses. 17

The party fully reunited, experimenting with the Blackspire's magic for a bit. They then made their way back to the rotting village; along the way, a death kiss attacked the party, killing Drangea and nearly killing Hark. Drangea learned the location of the Sanctuary of Eilistraee before being revived with a scroll. They rested in the rotting village before heading out, finding a dead farmer cursed by Agatha and a dark spring. Its darkness consumed Drangea, leading to her attacking the party before fleeing into the nearby woods.

3 October, 2025 - Ses. 16

Hark and Drangea discussed Hark's nightmares with Agatha, and she promised to stop the nightmares in exchange for killing the Rotlord. They set out for the rotting village, fighting ghosts and a dark chimera along the way. They also found a guillotine with many heads and a cursed crown, also attempting to convince a random passerby to approach the guillotine. they arrived in the rotting village, buying candles from a girl selling supplies. They entered a temple where they met Michael the Rotlord who allowed them to rest and told them where to find Agatha's hut. They journeyed to the nearby Blackspire, a huge crystal spire which repels magic while absorbing darkness.

26 September, 2025 - Ses. 15

Our heroes faced off against and defeated a hydra from a bog, harvesting some parts of it. They then crossed the Thunderbog River, with Sebastian turning into a shark to carry the party across. They entered the Twisting Darkwood, finding an old mushroom stand with a cursed amulet that Hark put on. More monsters attacked the party, and they found a strange tree with pitch black darkness in its crooked mouth. After some experimentation, our heroes attempted to destroy the tree which fought back, although it was ultimately defeated. After a long rest, Hark was unable to rest due to nightmares. A strange nurse arrived to ask about his condition, eventually being discovered as Agatha the hag.

5 September, 2025 - Ses. 14

Malrik Kaelthorn joined the party. Our heroes then faced off against the Warrior Sisters, pushing their way through to victory and gaining passage forward. Upon entering the Fumewoods, they bought mushrooms from a strange old lady. In exploring, they found a strange attack site, a deep bog with a monster inside, and a strange well. The party took a long rest, though Malrik was unable to properly rest due to nightmares.

29 August, 2025 - Ses. 13

Our heroes faced off against Damon Vortex, and although they defeated him, Spike died due to an unlucky death save. Our heroes got their rewards, and Spike decided to stay dead in the afterlife. The party held a funeral for Spike who's corpse exploded after Drangea kept pouring one out on him. The party agreed to have Maximus watch over the town temporarily. Afterwards, the party went to scout out the Warrior Sisters, fighting some vampiric mists along the way, and declared their names and deeds.

Level 7 Attained.

22 August, 2025 - Ses. 12

Our heroes made preparations for the festival. Damon Vortex gave a speech and summoned his demons before fleeing. They fought off gnolls and demons, slaying both Kalimas and Vassamos in the process. The party spoke with Damon Vortex before he transformed into a hideous undead form to fight the party with.

8 August, 2025 - Ses. 11

Sebastian rested in the surrounding woods, facing off a toxic bog giant. Drangea joined back up and fought Nocturna in the Bloodcrest Spire. After a difficult fight, she offered a truce, handing over the remains and offering her help with the invasion. The party upgraded their pendants and made preparations for the festival day.

1 August, 2025 - Ses. 10

Our heroes faced the constructs on the ground floor of the Bloodcrest Spire, defeating them after some magical difficulties. They ascended the tower, facing off against more constructs in a hallway filled with fog, almost leading to Sebastian's death.

25 July, 2025 - Ses. 9

Our heroes met Spike who joined up with the party. They journeyed to the Bloodcrest Spire, fighting Sybil, a priestess of Orcus. After a difficult battle, they continued on, reaching the Spire. They forced their way into a central chamber to attack some constructs.

18 July, 2025 - Ses. 8

Our heroes fought Kalimas, defeating his minions but failing to prevent him from escaping. Hark used his divine powers to turn back an angry spirit and found a teleportation circle out of the lair. The party made further preparations before resting for the night. They went to speak with Father Alvin to discuss the demonic invasion. Father Alvin told the party about a nearby tower with remains capable of upgrading the party's blood pendants. They decided to investigate it, with Drangea leaving her weapons to be enchanted in the meantime.

11 July, 2025 - Ses. 7

Sebastian descended the secret passage and fought off a revived Salamander. Drangea arrived to help explore the lair. While exploring, they were attacked by statues, destroying them with ease. Sebastian found an old journal about Vassamos and Kalimas, two cambions devoted to the mayor. They then found a secret passage leading to a journal about Vortex's plans to attack nearby towns with demons, also learning Father Alvin was Vortex's blackmailer. They met Kalimas, and Drangea tried but ultimately failed to impersonate the mayor, then escaping. Upon escaping, the party briefly spoke with Orcus before escaping, ready to attack Kalimas.

20 June, 2025 - Ses. 6

Sebastian and Hark were attacked by a wraith on their way into town who wanted revenge for Omen's death. They entered town after dispatching of him. They spoke with the mayor who asked for help dealing with a nearby threat which turned out to be Vassamos the cambion once more. They fought him again, although he escaped once more, and were rewarded for their efforts. Before they went back to the Blackmount Citadel, Father Alvin gave them means to create a teleportation circle between the Citadel and Vyrmas which they set up near old temple ruins. The party then found a secret passage in Mayor Vortex's office leading down somewhere below.

13 June, 2025 - Ses. 5

Sebastian and Brook stole a painting of Violetta from the mayor's house to give to Violetta. After defeating a strange hulking bug-like creature, they gave the painting to her, gaining her stone rod. Mira attacked the party again, breaking the seal with her stone rod but dying in the fight. The party then fought Omen Vortex with Hark coming in mid-fight to help out, resulting in Omen's death. The party then collected their reward from the mayor and descended the elevator to Vyrmas.

Level 6 attained.

6 June, 2025 - Ses. 4

Sebastian and Ru stayed in town while Brook, Hark, and Robin entered the Catacombs, finding crimson mist blanketing the floor, a sign of Omen Vortex's growing power. After facing some undead, the party was turned back by a puzzle guarding access to Violetta. After some more exploring, the party fought Mira, although she escaped with her stone rod barring Omen's lair. The party retreated into town for a short rest and returned to the Catacombs, opening Violetta's tomb. There, she offered her stone rod if the party gave her something beautiful.

30 May, 2025 - Ses. 3

The remains of Salamander revived as the party fully reunited, and the party put him down for good with holy water created by Hark. The party fought some more undead, causing Sebastian to gain mummy rot in the process. Our heroes found Maximus and returned him to the Blackmount Citadel where they discussed the mayor. After some shennanigans, the party cured Sebastian's rot and prepared for exploring the Catacombs the next day.

23 May, 2025 - Ses. 2

After pillaging Vassamos' other treasures, the party returned to town, receiving a Letter of Recognition. After a couple days of travel, the party arrived in the Blackmount Citadel, learning of the mayor's corruption. Our heroes descended into the Catacombs, facing off some undead, including the remains of Salamander, a master of pyromancy in life, finishing the session off with a short rest.

16 May, 2025 - Ses. 1

Our heroes met in the small town of Crow's Rest where the mayor tasked them with dealing with a demonic presence on the edge of town. After an ambush of gnolls, they fought the cambion Vassamos. He escaped, leaving behind his spear.

9 May, 2025 - Ses. 0

We handled character creation and discussed the campaign rules.
